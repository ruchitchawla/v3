about

Dipinti Kapoor is an architectural designer, an environment artist and an immersion artist.
Her practice is a geographical, and disciplinary mix of designs, pursuant to a balance of innovation that meets the unique function of each client. She is savvy of regional vernacular expressions and has been experimenting with, AR, VR and Application design as new representative media.
Her recent work, studies and narrates, a saga of Deep Urbanism. A design collaborative between AI and Human Intelligence, where she is actively researching with ML tools: TensorFlow and PyTorch. Her work uses GIS, global surveillance AI and point cloud analytics, for representations and ML curation.
A curious professional, fascinated by the polygamous coexistence of Architecture, History and Technology. She is found furiously reading, writing or drawing/solving mazes when she is not vacantly staring into space drawing ideas mid-air.
